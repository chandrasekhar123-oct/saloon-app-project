Frequently Asked

Quick answers for our human community.

How does the OTP verification work?

The OTP is only generated once your service is fully complete. By giving it to the owner, you authorize the completion of the booking—ensuring you are happy with the results.

Can a Salon Owner also be a Stylist?

Absolutely! Owners can join their own shop as a worker, allowing them to manage their own clients and specialties while running the business.
